What I Changed

01

1 Page → 8 Pages

Before: A single-page site with a hero image, one testimonial, a basic contact form, and broken navigation links. No real content depth.
After: A full 8-page website: Home, Services, Gallery, Skin Quiz, Shop, About, Contact, and FAQ. Each page is purpose-built to convert visitors into clients.
02

Generic Template → Custom Brand Design

Before: Standard template feel with generic purple/indigo colors. Placeholder-style hero image. Basic layout with no visual identity.
After: Custom brand system with Cormorant Garamond and Montserrat fonts, a warm burgundy palette that matches "Little Red" branding, sparkle decorations, gradient text effects, and professional photography of the actual injector.
03

No SEO → Full SEO Optimization

Before: No meta descriptions, no Open Graph tags, no structured data, no keywords. Invisible to search engines.
After: Full SEO package: meta descriptions, Open Graph and Twitter cards, local business schema markup (JSON-LD), keyword-rich content targeting "nurse injector Utah," sitemap.xml, and robots.txt.
04

Static Content → Interactive Features

Before: Static text and images only. No interactive elements. No engagement features.
After: Interactive skin mapping tool where users tap facial zones to learn about treatments. Skin quiz that recommends personalized treatments. Scroll-triggered fade-in animations. Animated marquee strip. Floating "Book Now" button.
05

Broken Links → Full Functionality

Before: Broken navigation links. Empty social media URLs. Payment plans page that went nowhere. No booking integration.
After: Every link works. Vagaro booking integration on every page. Working social links (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok). Clickable phone number and email. Google Reviews link.
06

No Social Proof → Trust Built In

Before: One testimonial. No credentials shown. No before/after photos. No trust signals.
After: Google Reviews section with 5.0 rating badge. Three client testimonials. Before/after photo gallery. Credential badges (Licensed RN, 8+ Years, Russian Lip Technique). "Meet Your Injector" section with Celeste's story and qualifications.
