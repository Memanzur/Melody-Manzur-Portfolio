About Me

Abraham Lincoln said, "Give me six hours to chop down a tree and I will spend the first four sharpening the axe." That is exactly how I work. Before anything gets built, the foundation has to be right.

My Cybersecurity degree led me to build Boppi, a Chrome extension that stops sensitive data from leaking into AI chatbots like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. It is live on the Chrome Web Store right now.

I also help businesses fix their architecture and automate their workflows through vibe coding. I run a digital marketing business where I rebuild websites, manage social media, and set up SEO so everything works together. And I am building The Melody Method to teach people how to actually use AI. My goal is to sharpen your axe so you can cut down bigger trees.
